Paprika Schnitzel

Description

This is a simple recipe for Paprikaschnitzel. The recipe is calculated for 2 people and takes a total time of approximatelx 40 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 2 Escalopes of Turkey ~300g
  • 250g of Cream
  • 1 Paprika
  • 1 Onion
  • Rapeseed Oil
  • Salt, Pepper, hot and sweet Paprika Spice, Chili
  • Broth

Steps

  1. Wash and slice the turkey fillets and season with salt, pepper, chilli and paprika.
  2. Dice the onion and bell pepper.
  3. Brown the meat, onion and pepper in a frying pan. Add the broth and simmer for 10-15 minutes with the lid on.
  4. Add the cream and simmer for another 5 minutes.
