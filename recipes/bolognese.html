Pasta Bolognese

Description

This is a simple recipe for Pasta Bolognese. The recipe is calculated for 4 people and takes a total time of approximatelx 35 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 500g of Pasta
  • 350g of Minced Meat
  • 250g of Sieved tomatoes
  • 1 Onion
  • 3 Cloves of Garlic
  • Half a Pepper
  • 1 Tomato
  • 1 Chili
  • Tomato paste
  • Rapeseed Oil
  • Salt, Pepper, Hot and Sweet Paprika Spice
  • Parmesan

Steps

  1. Bring the pasta water to the boil in a saucepan.
  2. Chop the onion, garlic, pepper, chilli and tomato into small pieces and fry in a pan with the tomato paste and oil.
  3. Season the mince with salt, pepper and paprika and add to the frying pan.
  4. Add the pasta to the boiling water.
  5. Add the tomato puree to the sauce.
  6. Serve with parmesan.
